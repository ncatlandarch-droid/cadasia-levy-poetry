2 Ownership & Intellectual Property

The Participant owns their brand, all creative works, website content, and brand assets. The Foundation holds zero equity, zero ownership stake, and zero revenue share in the Participant's business. The Participant may leave at any time and retain all materials.

What you own forever: All poems & written works · All original artwork · The "Words That Heal" brand name · Your name, likeness & persona · All audio recordings of your poetry.

3 Revenue Model

The Participant manages their own store and keeps 100% of all revenue from every stream:

Revenue StreamCreator Share
Merchandise sales (t-shirts, mugs, prints)100% of net profit
Digital products (audio collection, ebook)100% of net profit
Art print sales100% of net profit
Tips & donations (CashApp, Zelle)100% — directly to Creator
Plain English: You keep everything you earn. All revenue from merch, digital products, and art sales flows directly to your own Stripe account and your own bank. Tips and donations go straight to you. The Foundation takes zero cut, zero commission, and zero revenue share — ever. The only costs deducted are third-party platform fees (Printful production costs, Stripe processing fees) which are standard and unavoidable.

Why $0 cost and 0% revenue share?

All services are funded by Foundation grants and donations. The Foundation's mission is to launch underserved creators — not to profit from them. After your initial support period ends, you may choose a continued support plan:

4 Participant Obligations

Participant agrees that:

(a) All information provided is truthful and accurate;

(b) They are the sole creator of the works submitted for display and sale;

(c) They will maintain Active status by submitting quarterly reports via their admin dashboard;

(d) They will approve merch designs before production;

(e) They will manage their own store, including product listings, pricing, and customer communications;

(f) They will not misrepresent the Think! Ventures relationship.

5 Store Setup & Merch Development

Think! Ventures Foundation designs and sets up the Participant's store as part of the initial launch:

• Creates branded merch designs (t-shirts, mugs, prints, etc.) using the Participant's brand assets

• Sets up the Participant's own Printful account and Stripe account

• Uploads all designs, configures products, and syncs the store to the website

• Provides step-by-step guides for managing the store

Ownership: The Participant owns their own Printful store, Stripe account, and all revenue. Payments go directly to the Participant's bank account. The Foundation never handles or holds Participant revenue.

Ongoing management: The Participant is responsible for managing their own store after launch. Order fulfillment is handled automatically by Printful (print-on-demand, zero inventory, ships directly to customers).

New merch development: The Participant may request additional merch designs at any time. Think! Design & Planning will create new designs, process them to print-ready specifications, and upload them to the Participant's store. New merch requests are included during the initial support period and available through the Alumni Plan ($150/month) or Creator Plan ($500/month) after that period ends.

6 Engagement & Archival

Active status is maintained by submitting quarterly reports through the built-in admin dashboard on the Participant's website. Reports include a brief update on creative output and any store activity. Participants who show no activity for 90 consecutive days and do not respond to two outreach attempts will be moved to Inactive status (reduced support). After 180 days of inactivity, services may be archived with 30 days prior notice. Reactivation is available at any time by contacting the Foundation.

7 Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 90 days written notice. The Foundation may terminate immediately for cause including illegal activity, fraud, harassment, or misrepresentation.

Upon termination, Participant keeps their brand, creative works, and content. All documents are transferred and data exported to the Participant. No penalties. No fees. No strings.

8 Limitation of Liability

The Foundation provides creative platform services and guidance, not legal or financial advice. Participants are responsible for their own creative and business decisions. The Foundation is not liable for business outcomes. Participants are encouraged to consult an attorney for legal matters and a CPA for tax matters.

9 Case Study Permission

By signing this agreement, Participant grants the Foundation permission to feature their brand as a case study in marketing materials, grant applications, and public communications. The Foundation will seek approval before publication and will anonymize details upon request.

10 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Foundation and the Participant regarding the services described herein. It may be amended only in writing signed by both parties.
