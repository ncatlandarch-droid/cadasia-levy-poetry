1. Executive Summary

Words That Heal LLC is a poetry, art, and creative merchandise brand founded by Cadasia Ta'Nae Levy of Rocky Mount, North Carolina. The company publishes original poetry, sells original artwork and gallery-quality art prints, and offers branded merchandise through an online store — all built around a mission of healing through creative expression.

The brand operates through a multilingual website (English, Spanish, Chinese, Swahili) with integrated e-commerce, serving a global audience of poetry lovers, art collectors, and supporters of independent Black female artists.

$0 Startup Cost
100% Creator-Owned
5+ Revenue Streams
4 Languages
Key differentiator: Words That Heal combines deeply personal poetry with original visual art and branded merchandise — an integrated creative brand, not just a poetry page. The multilingual platform and professional production quality position the brand for both domestic and international markets.

2. Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To transform pain into beauty through raw, honest poetry and art — creating a space where healing begins with words and self-expression becomes a pathway to empowerment.

Vision

To build a nationally recognized creative brand rooted in Rocky Mount, NC that inspires healing through poetry, art, and community — proving that powerful stories from small towns can reach the world.

3. Founder Profile

Cadasia Ta'Nae Levy is a poet, visual artist, and creative entrepreneur from Rocky Mount, North Carolina. Her work explores themes of love, loss, identity, faith, and self-discovery — drawing from deeply personal experiences to create art that resonates universally.

Cadasia's artistic style blends intricate ink drawings — featuring dragons, florals, feminine portraits, and ornate lettering — with emotionally charged poetry that reads like spoken word on the page. Her unique combination of visual art and written word distinguishes her from traditional poets and visual artists alike.

Creative range: Original poetry (5+ published works) · Ink & pen illustration · Tattoo-inspired fine art · Abstract color compositions · Typographic art · Spoken word performance

5. Market Opportunity

Industry Overview

The poetry and spoken word market has experienced a renaissance driven by social media, with "Instapoetry" artists like Rupi Kaur selling millions of copies. The print-on-demand market is projected to reach $39.4 billion by 2030, with personalized and artist-driven products leading growth.

6. Revenue Model

Words That Heal operates a multi-stream, 100% creator-owned revenue model:

Revenue StreamPlatformCreator ShareAvg. Margin
Merchandise (apparel, mugs, prints)Printify100%55-80%
Digital products (ebook, audio)Gumroad90% (10% platform fee)90%
Art print salesPrintify100%70-80%
Physical book salesPrintify / Direct100%65-70%
Tips & donationsCashApp100%100%
Revenue ownership: Cadasia keeps 100% of all revenue from every stream. All payments flow directly to her business bank account (Novo). The Foundation takes zero cut, zero commission, and zero equity — ever.

Pricing Strategy

Products are priced at premium-but-accessible levels, reflecting the quality of original art and poetry while remaining affordable for the target audience. Merch margins of 55-80% are achieved through print-on-demand efficiency (no inventory, no warehousing, no shipping labor).

8. Operations & Fulfillment

Business Model: Print-on-Demand

Words That Heal operates a zero-inventory, print-on-demand model. This eliminates the need for upfront product investment, warehousing, or shipping logistics.

StepWho Does ItWhat Happens
1. Customer ordersCustomerVisits website → selects product → pays via Stripe
2. Order routes to PrintifyAutomaticOrder sent to print partner automatically
3. Product printedPrintify networkItem printed/produced within 2-5 business days
4. Shipped to customerPrintify partnerDirect-to-consumer shipping with tracking
5. Payment depositedAutomaticRevenue deposited to Novo business account
Founder's daily role: Create content, engage on social media, respond to customer messages, and approve new product designs. All production, shipping, and payment processing is automated.

Digital Product Delivery

Digital products (ebook, audio collection, digital art) are delivered instantly through Gumroad. Customer pays → receives download link immediately → no shipping required.
