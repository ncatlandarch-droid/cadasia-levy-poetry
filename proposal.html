Ownership & Rights

Your creative work belongs to you — always. You grant Think! Ventures a license to display and sell your work, which you can revoke at any time with 30 days notice.

What You Own (Forever)

  • All poems and written works
  • All original artwork
  • The "Words That Heal" brand name
  • Your name, likeness, and persona
  • All audio recordings of your poetry

What Think! Ventures Manages

  • Website code & design
  • Store infrastructure
  • Marketing & business strategy
  • Payment processing systems
  • Order fulfillment logistics

Your Responsibilities:

• Keep creating poems and art — your content is the engine
• Approve merch designs before production
• Share your website on social media and with your network
• Respond to communications within 48 hours

What You Don't Have To Do:

• No managing the website  • No handling customer orders  • No processing payments  • No dealing with technology  • No paying any fees

Section 04
Term & Termination

This agreement is effective for 24 months from the date of signing. After 24 months, terms may be renegotiated by mutual agreement.

Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

If you choose to leave:

• Website stays live for 30 days for transition
• All creative content returned to you
• Pending revenue paid within 15 business days
• Merch products removed from the store

If Think! Ventures terminates:

• Website files and content transferred to you
• Training provided to manage your own store
• All pending revenue paid within 15 business days

"This is a partnership, not a contract to trap you. If anything is unclear, ask before you sign."
